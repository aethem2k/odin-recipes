Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

nad_dyagileva Pixabay

Description

Easily-made cookies featuring the crunch of dark chocolate chips and the aroma of cinnamon - great as a quick, rich snack

Ingredients

  • Vegetable milk
  • Dark chocolate chips
  • Flour
  • Seed oil
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients with the oil
  2. Stir the mixture while gradually adding milk until desired consistency is reached
  3. Shape cookies out of the dough and lay them on a parchment-lined baking sheet
  4. Put the baking sheet in the oven and bake for approximately 30 minutes at 180 degrees C
