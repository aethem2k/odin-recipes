Description

A tasty and reliable recipe, high in protein

Ingredients

  • Seitan
  • Soy sauce
  • Bell pepper
  • Oil
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Cook the seitan and the vegetables in a greased skillet until browned
  2. Add the soy sauce and cook for 5 more minutes
