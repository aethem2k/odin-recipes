Cucumber and Avocado Sushi

Einladung_zum_Essen

Pixabay

Description

A fresh, lightweight classic of Japanese and Fusion cuisine

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Soy sauce (optional)
  • Nori sheets
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil
  2. Cook the rice
  3. Slice the vegetables
  4. With the help of a sushi mat, make rolls out of the nori sheet, the rice and the vegetables
  5. Slice the rolls into 6 pieces each
